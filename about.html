Bio

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto, Canada. My research examines the role of technology in worker resistance and unionization amidst the platformization of urban life. Specifically, I investigate how digital tools can support unions and workers in location-based labour platforms to organize around and resist/confront the impacts of climate change on their working conditions.

My interests include worker-centric platform designs, tech-mediated resistance in social movements, and critical studies of caste and technology. Prof. Priyank Chandra advises my doctoral studies.
