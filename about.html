About Me

Background

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto, Canada. My research examines the role of technology in worker resistance and unionisation amidst the platformisation of urban life.

Specifically, I investigate how digital tools can support unions and workers in location-based labour platforms to organise around and resist/confront the impacts of climate change on their working conditions.

Read Full Bio

Research Interests

  • Worker-centric platform designs
  • Tech-mediated resistance in social movements
  • Critical studies of caste and technology
  • Climate change impacts on labor

My doctoral studies are advised by Prof. Priyank Chandra.
