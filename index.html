About Ashique

I am a Ph.D. student in the Faculty of Information at the University of Toronto, Canada, studying the role of technology in worker resistance and unionization within digital labor platforms. My interests also include tech-mediated resistance in social movements, alternative platform designs, and critical studies of caste and technology. Prof. Priyank Chandra advises my doctoral studies.
