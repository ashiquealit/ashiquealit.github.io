Ashique Ali Thuppilikkat

Labour & Social Computing

Ashique Ali Thuppilikkat (he/him) is a PhD student at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto, and a researcher at STREET Lab. His research focuses on the role of technology in worker resistance and unionisation amidst the platformization of urban life. He examines how digital tools can support unions and workers on location-based digital labour platforms to organise and resist the impacts of algorithmic management.
