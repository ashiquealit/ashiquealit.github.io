Ashique Ali Thuppilikkat

PhD Candidate in Information Studies, University of Toronto
Connaught PhD Fellow for Public Impact Scholarship (15/07/2025)

I am a researcher focusing on digital labor, social movements, and platform economies, with a regional interest in South Asia. My work explores resistance, solidarity, and cooperative models in the context of neoliberal urbanism and digital platforms.
