Ashique Ali Thuppilikkat

PhD Candidate in Information Studies, University of Toronto

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto, Canada. My research examines the role of technology in worker resistance and unionization amidst the platformization of urban life. Specifically, i investigates how digital tools can support unions and workers in location-based labour platforms to organize around and resist/confront the impacts of climate change on their working conditions. My interests also include worker-centric platform designs, tech-mediated resistance in social movements, and critical studies of caste and technology. Prof. Priyank Chandra advises my doctoral studies.
