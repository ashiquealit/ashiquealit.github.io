About Me

I am a PhD student at the Faculty of Information, University of Toronto, researching the role of technology in worker resistance and unionization amidst platformization. My work explores digital tools to support workers in location-based labor platforms, addressing climate change impacts on working conditions. Advised by Prof. Priyank Chandra, my interests span worker-centric platform designs and critical studies of caste and technology.
